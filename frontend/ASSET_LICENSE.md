# Hidden Tower Defence game assets

All visual assets in this frontend are original project-owned work.

The tile palette, castle geometry, characters, arrows, scrolls, beacons, and
effects are generated locally by `src/assets.ts` and `src/TowerScene.ts`.
They do not incorporate or download third-party art, fonts, textures, or
runtime assets.
